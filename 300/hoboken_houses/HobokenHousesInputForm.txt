N lines of the form (x, y) where x,y are integers that indicate the displacement from Keith's current position to his new position.
Keith walks along the wall in straight lines.
Keith starts at the origin. It is guaranteed that at the end of N lines, Keith will return to the origin.
Keith's path has no self-intersections.

1 <= N <= 2^7, -(2^32) <= x,y <= (2^32)
